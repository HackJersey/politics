About

This site was built in a single hack day organized by Hack Jersey, using the Sunlight Foundation's Influence Explorer API.

The project was part of Hack Jersey's "Law, Money & Politics." event at NJIT on Sept. 21, 2013. You can read all about our process in this blog post.

If you'd like to review the code, you can see our politics hack day repo on Github. Feel free to fork it and send us a pull request.

We'd like to thank our hack day sponsors, especially Broadstreet Ads, Sunlight Foundation and Sunlight Labs, NJIT, the New Jersey News Commons and the Hoboken MakerBar.

The hack day team that worked on this project included Samuel Carvalho, John Crepezzi, Josh Engelberg, Eric Hellman, Kenny Katzgrau, Margaret Kim, Joseph Kokenge, Yvonne Leow, Tom Meagher, Patrick Murray, Eric Sagara, Benjamin Scalera, Sebastian Serrano, Kimi Wei.

Our other contributors who helped plan the event and this project were Fred Kaimann, Bert Hartmann, Marco Chang and Carlos Paulino.

If you'd like to get involved with Hack Jersey, our journalists and programmers, we'd love to have you. Reach out and join us!
